Easy Taco Rice

    Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion, diced
  • 4 Cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 large green pepper, diced
  • 1 large tomato, diced
  • 200ml cooking oil
  • 500g ground beef (can substitute ground chicken or crumbled tofu**
  • 350 ml shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 tablespoons or 1 pack of taco seasoning
  • 1 & 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cups prepared white rice (please avoid instant rice)
  • Optional:1 chili pepper or chili pepper flakes for heat

    Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat 100ml oil over medium heat
  2. Brown ground beef or turkey and drain excess juices
  3. ** for crumbled tofu, add to the oil and stir-fry for 3 minutes until lightly browned
  4. Add 1 cup water and taco seasoning to the cooked meat (or tofu) and let simmer for 3 minutes, stirring frequently
  5. Stir in the diced onions and garlic (and chili) and cook for five minutes or until the onion is translucent
  6. Stir in the diced pepper and simmer until soft and fragrant, around 1-2 minutes
  7. Lower the mixture to a low simmer and mix in the cooked white rice
  8. Add the remaining 1/2 cup water and diced tomato and let simmer for 5 minutes to let the flavors FLOW
  9. Remove from heat and mix in the shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Enjoy! Best served hot with a light dolup of sour cream or hot sauce of choice
