The Amazing Jimmy Sammy

    Ingredients

  • Large foot long sub or french bread, sliced in two
  • deli style munster cheese slices
  • deli style swiss cheese slices
  • Deli style turkey or meat of choice
  • Pickled or fresh sliced Jalapenos
  • cream cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Balsamic Italian Dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional
  • Lettuce, tomato and onion.

    Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to broil
  2. Slice your bread of choice horizontally and place both pieces face up on a oven pan
  3. Soak both halves with a light portion of Balsamic italian Dressing or vinegarette. Only a slight amount will do as we don't want to make the bread soggy.
  4. Toast on broil for three minutes or until lightly toasted.
  5. With one half of the bread, spread a generous amount of cream cheese on the open face of the bread.
  6. With the remaining half, spread a generous amount of mayonnaise to taste.
  7. Layer meat and cheese as desired, preferably in equal amounts
  8. If adding vegetables it is advisable to place all desired veggies on the top bun as desired along with the Jalapenos
  9. Apply a light drizzle of yellow mustard and salt and pepper (to tatse)
  10. Assemble the two halves in a stack, and cut into portion sizes of choice.
  11. Enjoy!
