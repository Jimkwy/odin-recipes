Gouchijiang Stir-fry

Recipes

  • Gouchijiang Srir-fry
  • The Amazing Jimmy Sammy
  • Easy Taco Rice

    Ingredients

  • 1 large sized onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 100ml of cooking oil or butter
  • 2 chicken breasts or 1 large firm block of tofu
  • 1 large green pepper
  • Optional: Red chili or Red Chili flakes for added spice

    • The Sauce

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 100ml Gouchijiang red chilli paste
  • 100ml Ketchup

    Instructions

  1. In a large pan heat oil on medium heat
  2. Add slivered onion and cook until browned
  3. Add garlic and cook for one minute
  4. Add gouchijiang paste and cook until fragrant
  5. While stirring add chicken broth and ketchup
  6. Mix in diced peppers and tofu cut into small equally sized cubes
  7. let simmer for 10 to 15 minutes or until the sauce reaches a thick consistency
